THEOREM 3.1 (EPS Linear):
  ||x̂^N - x̂^1|| ≤ εH² (e^(LH) - 1)/(LH) · (1 + 1/N)
  Where:
    ε = curvature bound
    H = interval length
    L = Lipschitz constant
    N = number of steps
